NorthBright FindURL Reviews
Simple website sniffer that runs scans in a built-in browser and finds all downloadable resources when it comes to audio, video, image, text and Flash content
FindURL automatically detects resource URLs on a visited website, giving you the possibility to download audio, video, image, text and Flash content.
This software application has a built-in browser and customizable filters. It doesn't require plugins or drivers, and it doesn't affect your browser's performance.
Simple installation and UI
The setup procedure is fast and shouldn't give you any trouble. It's wrapped in a clean and intuitive interface, which consists of a large window with buttons for detecting audio, video, image, text, Flash content or all, as well as for setting up filters to look only for particular types of files, such as .mp3, .avi or .jpg.
